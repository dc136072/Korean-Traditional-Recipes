Korean Beef Bulgogi

Ingredients

  • Korean-cut frozen beef slices
  • Korean Pear
  • Onion and Garlc
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Rice wine
  • Brown sugar
  • Black pepper
  • Sesame Oil

Description

A classic Korean BBQ beef that is thinnly sliced and marinated in a flavorful soysauce base. Its a tender and juicy dish that would make your mouth water once you lay your eyes on it. There are many varieties of this tradional dish; todays focus is the Classic Bulgogi.

Recipe credits:

  • Beyondkimchee - Blog

Steps

  1. Put diced pear, onion, and garlic in a blender until smooth.
  2. Tranfer the puree into a container.
  3. Add soy sauce, rice wine, brown sugar, black pepper, sesame oil into the puree and mix well.
  4. Let the mixture sit in the refrigerator for a week or a few days to marinate.
  5. Dap the beef dry and separte the meat slices; then putting it into the marinade sauce.
  6. Gently massage and cover the meat with the sauce throughly and cover it in a refigerator for at least 1 hour.
  7. Heat up a skillet to high heat and add the beef. Do not add vegetable oil since the heat will render out oil from the fat within the beef slices.
  8. Cook the meat evenly and thoroughly, about 2 to 3 minutes each side.
  9. Add chopped green onions and sesame seeds for more flavor and aesthetics.
